Description

Prepare for adrenaline-fueled drifting action in Drift Hunters! This game puts you behind the wheel of high-performance drift cars as you compete in intense drift battles. With stunning graphics and realistic physics, immerse yourself in the world of drifting as you master the art of controlled slides around tight corners and challenging courses. Upgrade and customize your cars to enhance performance and style, and dominate the drift scene like never before!

Controls:

Use the arrow keys to steer your car.
Press the spacebar to brake or reverse.
Hold down the shift key for a temporary boost of speed.
Use the "Z" key to initiate a drift.
Press the "X" key to brake drift and straighten your car.

Tips and tricks:

  • Practice throttle control to maintain a smooth and controlled drift.
  • Experiment with different cars and setups to find the perfect balance of speed and control.
  • Master the timing of your drift initiation and exit to maximize points and maintain momentum.

About the creator:

Drift Hunters is developed by StanceWorks, a passionate team dedicated to creating immersive drifting experiences.
